About Wabel Medical Inc.

A certified independent biomedical service company based in the Greater Toronto Area.

Our Mission

Keeping Your Clinic Running

Wabel Medical Inc. was founded on a simple principle: clinics deserve fast, reliable, independent equipment service — not long waits in a corporate queue.

As a certified biomedical technician, we specialize in the repair, calibration, and maintenance of physiotherapy and rehabilitation equipment. With hands-on experience across a wide range of devices and manufacturers, we bring expert-level service directly to your clinic.

Being independent means we're not tied to any single brand or distributor — we service any equipment you own, from any manufacturer, with no restrictions and no bias.
