Who We Are

About Wabel Medical Inc.

Professional biomedical technician services for physiotherapy modalities and medical devices across the Greater Toronto Area.

Our Expertise

Specialists in Physiotherapy Equipment Service

Wabel Medical Inc. specializes in the calibration and maintenance of physiotherapy medical devices. Our team of certified biomedical technicians brings decades of combined experience in ensuring medical equipment operates at peak performance and safety standards.


We understand that reliable, accurately calibrated equipment is essential for effective patient treatment. Our comprehensive service approach combines technical expertise with a deep understanding of clinical workflows, ensuring minimal disruption to your practice.


From ultrasound and laser therapy devices to treatment tables and shockwave equipment, we service all major brands and models used in physiotherapy and rehabilitation settings.

Coverage Area

Serving All of Ontario

We offer full coverage across Ontario, including the GTA and surrounding regions. Our mobile service units are equipped with professional-grade calibration equipment and diagnostic tools, allowing us to perform comprehensive service visits at your location.

This eliminates the need to transport sensitive medical equipment and reduces downtime. We serve clients across Ontario with fast, reliable on-site service.

Whether you're in Toronto, the surrounding GTA, or anywhere across Ontario, we're ready to support your facility's equipment maintenance needs.
