Specialized Device Services

IFC & TENS Devices

Comprehensive service for interferential current and transcutaneous electrical nerve stimulation units. Our technicians verify output parameters, electrode integrity, and safety compliance for optimal therapeutic results.

Our Process

What's Included in Every Service Visit

Every service visit from Wabel Medical Inc. follows a thorough, standardized process to ensure your equipment is fully compliant and performing optimally.

🔍

Full Diagnostics

Comprehensive assessment of all device functions, outputs, and components to identify any issues before they become problems.

📐

Precision Calibration

Using professional-grade calibration tools to ensure all output parameters meet manufacturer and regulatory specifications.

🛠️

Repair & Replacement

On-the-spot repair of identified issues including component replacement, cable repairs, and applicator servicing.

📋

Certified Documentation

Full written calibration report provided after every visit — the documentation your clinic needs for compliance and accreditation.
